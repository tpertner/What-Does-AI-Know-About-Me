## About the Talk

A 15-minute lightning talk walking through:

1. **The Problem** — how much sensitive data employees are feeding into AI
2. **The Framework** — a 3-layer model for auditing AI data exposure (Surface, Behavioral, System)
3. **Platform Comparison** — how ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity handle your data
4. **The Business Risk** — Samsung, shadow AI breaches, and the regulatory landscape
5. **Take Action** — three tiers of response (individual, team, organization)
6. **Live Demo** — running the audit prompt on a real ChatGPT account
7. **References** — full citations for every stat and example
8. **Close** — how to get started with this toolkit

## Want to Run This Talk Yourself?

The slides are shared under the same MIT license as the rest of the repo. You're welcome to adapt them for your own organization, team, or conference — attribution appreciated but not required.

A few notes if you do:

- **The deck is designed for a 15-minute slot** with about 2 minutes for the live demo. Adjust timing as needed.
- **The live demo is the most memorable moment** — don't skip it. Run the audit prompt on your own account before presenting so you know what to expect.
- **Update the stats before presenting.** AI research moves fast; the numbers in this deck are current as of April 2026.
- **Match it to your audience.** The version here is tuned for a mixed executive/practitioner crowd. For a pure technical audience, you may want to go deeper on the platform-specific mechanics.
